About POwithV








POwithV is a name for pre-order service or shopping entrusted goods service in Indonesia. POwithV is formed in October 29, 2008 and based on Tangerang, Indonesia. We offers pre-order for food and medicine. We have an entrusted partners like an official outlet or Reseller.









Our website always available everytime and everyday but our physical service such a
Customer Service or our outlet or our service for shipping your order will start
open from 09:00 until 22:00. We also don't serve shipping on sunday










We work with shipment service to ship your order. We work with several
Shipment Corporation such as JNE, Si Cepat, Gojek, and we also serve shipping
abroad. We keep trying to expand our shipping range to make you easier if you
want to use our services.
