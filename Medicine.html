Allopurinol is a drug used to reduce uric acid levels in the blood.

increase due to chemotherapy treatment in cancer patients. High levels

stone formation.


Category C: Studies in animal studies have shown adverse effects on
the fetus, but there have been no controlled studies in pregnant women.
